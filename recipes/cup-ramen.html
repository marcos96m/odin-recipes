Cup Ramen



How to cook ramen

First you open the cup of ramen,inside you'll find a spice packet. Take the spice packet out and add water. Put the cup ramen in your microwave. Heat for about 3 min. Once done heating, take out and add the contents of the spice packet and add to the ramen. Let cool for a bit then enjoy eating.

Ingredients

  • cup ramen
  • water
  • microwave

Steps

  1. Get cup ramen
  2. Open cup ramen and take out spice packet
  3. Put water into cup ramen
  4. Put the cup ramen into the microwave for about 3 minutes
  5. Add sodium packet to the ramen and mix
  6. wait for it to cool then enjoy
